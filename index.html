FAQ

What is LILY SELF-CARE all about?
LILY SELF-CARE is a wellness platform offering tools and guidance for mindfulness, healthy living, and emotional clarity through curated practices and digital resources.
Are the meditation sessions beginner-friendly?
Yes! All mindfulness sessions are designed for beginners, with step-by-step guidance to help you build a consistent practice.
Do I need to pay for journal templates or self-care checklists?
No, all downloadable resources including journal templates, trackers, and checklists are available for free.
Are guest speaker sessions recorded?
Yes, replays of guest expert talks are uploaded and accessible in our resource library.
What if I miss a daily or weekly challenge?
No worries! You can join any challenge at your own pace. Consistency matters more than perfection.
